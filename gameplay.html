UBISOFT VALIANT HEARTS SITE

Valiant Hearts is a side-scrolling, puzzle-based, adventure video game which takes place during World War I. The game comprises four chapters split into several sections. Most of these involve solving puzzles by obtaining certain items needed for the situation. Other sections include wartime segments requiring the player to survive heavy gunfire, stealth sections where the player must avoid being detected by enemies, and rhythmic car chase sections set to classic songs. Each character can interact with objects, perform a melee attack to knock out guards or smash through debris, and throw projectiles. There are also some traits unique to each character, which better to explore by yourself with this game.

Platforms: PC, PlayStation 3, PlayStation 4, Xbox 360, Xbox One, iOS, Android and Nintendo Switch.
